Welcome to Acid Reign...

Skwad Reign

What is Acid Reign?

Acid Reign is Skwad’s apersonal digitized space where shit happens and doesn’t. We’re here to build apokalyptic hellscapes splashed with neodadaesque memetics and more. This is a space to capture the essence of what was Nobody™ & Telecomix. Here’s where Debordian play is set free within the infinite constraints of the Dérive. Simply, Acid Reign is a personal space for the impersonal.

What are the Goals of Acid Reign?

The goals of Acid Reign are to manipulate and alter what’s already present physically. To weave onto the fabric of humanity’s collective information space, to leave our shadowy mark on this collective. For a time to remain anon, in the long to produce a sense of sorrow & withdrawl within our courpsey art. The goal of Acid Reign is to convert and translate, to redistribute ideology with express purpose of freeing and suffocating the individual. To repeat endlessly our methods in constant idolatry. Slowly changing our methods in accordance with Hegelian motion. Our end is expression and repression. One flame perishes and another rises with the flint. The bow tied against two ends, we are the body.

Acid Reign's Political Views

None. However, if you must enforce labels, all of them to the left. Our ideas shape against the window of the material. We follow the traces that history has left for us to construct a better future. Our organization is intersectional, worker focused, and for all the downtrodden pushed down by institutional oppression. We seek to abolish capitalism and to socialize capital goods and the results of production. However, the methods we take to do this bubble in the cauldron of our collective. Some of our kin merely oppose the present system, while others have stewed in the pot longer. Our series of present moments has since been our manifesto, but manifested henceforth will be the tangible objects of our ideology. Each piece we etch onto the annals of though will be more biting, more thorough, more frightening than the last, as we dig closer out of our chained caves.

Endgame

We seek to negate E.Y.E and build a future worth living in. To produce a time of genuine enthusiasm for one’s place and all places, for all places cradle humanity. We wish to turn the natural resources of the Earth toward everyone. We want our current level of production to grow to eliminate as much labor as possible. We wish to free play, free exploration, free grey oppressive walls from private property and give it unto art. Every waking moment should be delirious expression realized. We say, “Do what thou wilt”, but we are not Thelemites. We are artists, vagabonds, spacklers of slammed holes, thieves, stalkers, misfits, rogue painters, burglars, the gaps in between prison bars, we are the light in the basement, and the water for hydroponics. Our cause is universal, our claim is the throne, our table will be as wide as needed to fit all.
